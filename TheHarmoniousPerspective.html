The Harmonious Perspective

The first step, therefore, on this extraordinary path is the Harmonious Perspective , commonly translated as Right View , or Right understanding . In other words, we start by explaining to the follower what the problem is, the cause of the problem , and the solution to the problem . In other words, we explain what should be done, why it should be done, and how it should be done. This means we explain , so no one can complain . In other words, this is not a “monkey see, monkey do” method. We speak to intelligent people who want to understand what they are doing, and why they are doing it.
