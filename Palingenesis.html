Palingenesis

Palingenesis (Palin = again + genesis = origination) is a term similar to re-birth, though often used in the same sense as metempsychosis, involving a soul. Punar-janman is also a term having a meaning similar to rebirth ( punar = again + janman = birth) but often equated to metempsychosis that involves a soul. The term punabbhava meaning re-being ( puna = again + bhava = being or existence) carries the same meaning as rebirth, though this term, like rebirth, is used in Buddhism with a special meaning.
