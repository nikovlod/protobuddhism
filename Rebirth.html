Rebirth

“Rebirth,” is a term used by the Buddhists in a very special sense. For the Buddhist, “rebirth” means "a new birth after death." It is the birth of a body and a mind, without a soul being involved. No “soul” leaves the body at death; no “soul” enters the new body at birth; yet the new body and mind is the result of the old body and mind. Buddhists do not believe in a soul. Therefore rebirth is not explained as the transmigration of a 'soul' from one body to another.
